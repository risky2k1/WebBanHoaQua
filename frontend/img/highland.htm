Description

Highlands Coffee Mobile Application is presented with the aim to serve deliciously bold cups of coffee and tea better and closer with more than millions of guest every year – bring to you special offers:

FASCINATING PROMOTIONS
Start with 50% OFF when downloading and registering app, more promotions made specifically for you will appear in “Rewards” section, turn on your notification and enjoy.

REWARD FOR “DRIPS”
Create account, collect “drips” for each purchase and receive rewards for Highlands Coffee members.

CONVENIENCE FEATURES
Along with promotion, Highlands Coffee App contains many convenience features that help you experience our service much easier such as Online Pre-Order, Delivery and Simple Payment via Prepaid Account.
